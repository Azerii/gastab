About Us

Fueling the world and igniting the future.

Gastab Global Haulage is a fast growing integrated
Petroleum Products supplying
and retailing company.
Registered in Nigeria with the aim of providing efficient and

yet affordable petroleum solution. Gastab Global Haulage has
been in existence for
about 3 decades now, which our sole
service was purely based on the distribution
of Petroleum
products across the nation.

Our past businesses, readiness for change and fortitude to
deliver on schedule has
not only prove our Integrity
and accountability but has also promote a sustainable

development for us, our diligent enthusiasm to overcome
challenges and flagship
brand is incomparable putting us in
the spotlight.

Gastab global Haulage partnering with several companies in
the oil sector such
as Mobil, NNPC, Total, & many other Oil
companies with Retails Service Stations
within Nigeria has
since then grown to become a leading force to reckon
with in
the forefront of the petroleum products industries in Nigeria.

Currently operating retail plants in several locations in
Nigeria, also available at our
plants are other products such
as AGO (diesel), DPK, LPG and Lubricants. Gastab
global
Haulage is able to supply both bulk, packed and Retail
format.

Gastab global Haulage also operates its own fleets with over
300 Tankers enabling
us to give our customers an
uninterrupted service.

Gastab Global Haulage has a growing multi-venture, we did
not limit our
responsibility and services for the good citizens
of the nation to only petroleum
products. We now have our
other services such as Gastab Auto Mart, Gastab

supermarket, Zamzam office complex and Kapital Kitchen.
